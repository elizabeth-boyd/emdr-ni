Elizabeth Boyd Counselling Service

Welcome to my Website and well done with taking the first step towards dealing with your problems.

I provide a relaxed, non-judgemental, confidential environment to explore the issues that are affecting you, help you to find ways to express yourself and deal with your private fears, woundings and situations. My aim is to walk with you and assist you to unravel your thoughts, challenge your perceptions, reveal possibilities, and strive to manage the circumstances we can find ourselves in from time to time, as well as those difficult dilemmas, tragedies and traumatic experiences that can sweep us off our feet.

I provide a variety of counselling approaches including CBT and EMDR (Eye Movement Desensitisation and Reprocessing) - Recommended by the National Institute for Health and Care Excellence for the treatment of trauma and PTSD. Sometimes we can feel unable to cope with the pace of life, and feel stressed or over-whelmed. Often we can find it difficult to verbalise what is causing us distress, it is my job to help you uncover the root source and solutions. I have a humorous, direct approach to my clients, coupled with a friendly, genuine empathy, creating a bespoke helping model of management strategies and techniques to help you reach your full potential.

Based around Banbridge, County Down, I have over 12 years Counselling experience and can provide the help you need. to get things back on track.
